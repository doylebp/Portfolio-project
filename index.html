ABOUT ME SECTION

I have a passion for teaching, and am especially interested in helping the very beginners! I have taken music lessons for over 25 years! On the piano, cello, up-right bass, and guitar. I have performed in many orchestras as a cellist, including the Millenial Choirs and Orchestra, the Cache Valley Symphony, the Utah Philharmonic Orchestra, and the University of Utah Campus Symphony. I have taught students in elementary school orchestra classes for 2 years, and have taught several of my own private students (both in my home and in theirs).
